Privacy Policy for BizLLCFinder

Last updated: December 2025

Introduction

Welcome to BizLLCFinder ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website at bizllcfinder.site.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Important Notice

BizLLCFinder does not collect, store, or process any personal information from our visitors. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and anonymity while using our services.

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information
  • Financial Information: We do not collect credit card numbers, bank account information, or any financial data
  • Location Data: We do not track your precise location or collect GPS coordinates
  • Social Media Information: We do not access your social media accounts or collect social media data
  • Communication Data: We do not read, store, or analyze your private communications

Information We May Collect

The only information we may collect is standard web analytics data that helps us understand how our website is used:

  • Usage Analytics: Basic information about how visitors use our website (pages visited, time spent, etc.)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, and general location (city/state level only)
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for website functionality (no tracking cookies)

How We Use Information

Any non-personal information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our website functionality and user experience
  • Analyze website performance and identify areas for improvement
  • Ensure our website works properly across different devices and browsers
  • Provide relevant LLC formation information and resources

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party services and resources:

  • External Links: We provide links to LLC formation services and government resources
  • Google Forms: Our contact form uses Google Forms (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • No Data Sharing: We do not share any information with third parties

Data Security

While we do not collect personal information, we maintain appropriate security measures to protect our website and any non-personal data we may process.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Contact us with any questions about our privacy practices
  • Request information about what data we may collect
  • Opt out of any analytics tracking (if applicable)
  • Report any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our contact form on the website.

Our Commitment

At BizLLCFinder, we believe in transparency and protecting your privacy. We are committed to maintaining a website that provides valuable LLC formation resources without compromising your personal information.
